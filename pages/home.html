Who are we?

Founded in 2017 at Foothill Ranch Library, Math at the Library is a free student-run math organization to help K-8th grade students improve their academic achievement in math. The tutors of the Math at the Library program are a group of high school students who are passionate about math and are willing to share our interests with young learners . We help students with math homework or provide additional challenging material for advanced learners.
