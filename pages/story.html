StudySmart Youth Services is a student-run nonprofit organization aimed to help K-8 students improve their academic achievements. The tutors of our program are dedicated high school volunteers who are passionate about learning and are eager to share their passions. Founded in 2017, it evolved from Math at the Library and has grown to encompass Code Champion , Girls Empowering Girls, and Mathmagicians. With online classes multiple times each week, we have over 200 volunteers and have served 2500 hours. We strongly belive in providing free lessons to students of all backgrounds and social status.
